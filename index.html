ABOUT

Greetings, kind visitor! My name is Sean Ryan, and I'm a front-end developer with a love for building exciting and useful web applications. While I'm always looking to learn new skills and technologies to push boundaries with what I create, my technical abilities rest on solid foundational knowledge of the time-honored trio at the core of the web: HTML, CSS and plain JavaScript.

My extensive professional background in marketing and communications makes me rather unique among technically-oriented folk. Owing to this experience, I pride myself on being able to speak to humans just as effectively as I speak to computers.

When I'm not coding, I'm usually playing guitar or drums in various bands that gig and record in and around New York City.
