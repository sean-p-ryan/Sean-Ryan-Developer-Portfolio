Technical Writer

I am an experienced technical writer with a long track record of distilling complex concepts into simple, clear, and useful content. I’m currently working at an enterprise B2B SaaS company where I create a diverse range of content including developer tool walkthroughs, implementation tutorials, demo videos, and sample applications to help engineers quickly learn and realize value from our product. I’m also a self-taught developer with strong underlying technical knowledge, coding proficiency, and a love of learning and working with new technologies.

In a nutshell, I like using words to solve problems between people and technology. I use this site to showcase some of the ways in which I have done that.

TECHNICAL WRITING

Whether I’m creating a feature guide, an installation tutorial, or a demo video, all of the technical content I create requires knowledge of three things: the concept (what am I writing about?), the reader (who am I writing for?), and the goal (what do I want this content to accomplish?). I see these three categories as the pillars of effective technical communication. If just one is missing, the whole piece will go astray.
